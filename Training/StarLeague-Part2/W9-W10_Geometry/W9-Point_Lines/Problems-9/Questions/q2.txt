Closest Pair Of Points (Starleague Training Problem)

Given N (2 <= N <= 100,000) distinct points in two-dimensional Euclidean space, find the square of the smallest 
distance between any two points. 

INPUT FORMAT (file mindist.in):
Line 1: N
Line 2 to N+1: Line i+1 contains two integers xi and yi (-1000,000 <= xi, yi <= 1000,000): coordinates of a point.

OUTPUT FORMAT (file mindist.out):
An integer: square distance between the closest pair of points.

SAMPLE INPUT
4
1 1
1 -1
-1 -1
-2 -2

SAMPLE OUTPUT
2
